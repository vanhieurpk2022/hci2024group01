Core Values

1. Creativity and Innovation: At BookingCare, we value the uniqueness of each product to create truly useful value for users, customers, and the community.
2. Competency Development through ASK: BookingCare personnel are shaped with the ASK Competency Triangle (Attitude, Skills, Knowledge) to independently think and grow their careers in any environment.
3. Culture of Lifelong Learning: At BookingCare, "learning goes hand in hand with doing." Our team is composed of young, proactive individuals who dare to take on challenges and continuously learn on the job.
4. Empowerment: At BookingCare, every member is empowered to take initiative in their work. This includes setting goals, planning tasks, and addressing difficulties proactively during the work process.
