About Us

HealthCare is a comprehensive healthcare platform that connects a network of leading hospitals, clinics, and healthcare services in Vietnam today. Through HealthCare, customers can easily select trusted and reputable doctors, medical facilities, and healthcare services from a rigorously verified and carefully curated network of medical institutions.

To date, through HealthCare, customers can access healthcare services from a network of over 200 reputable hospitals and medical facilities in Hanoi and Ho Chi Minh City, with more than 1,500 specialist doctors and thousands of services available on the system.

When searching on Google for healthcare information, a flood of results appears. Among them, there is a lot of unreliable, plagiarized, and unverified content that raises significant concerns.

HealthCare's Handbook has been developed with the mission of sharing reliable, accurate, and practical information verified for both its content and real-life experiences of medical examinations and healthcare services. These are also the core values that our team consistently upholds as principles for building our content platform.

Healthcare is a field that demands accurate, reliable, and well-vetted information. The information that readers seek about healthcare generally falls into two categories.

First, content sharing experiences and real-life accounts of medical examinations and healthcare, which are trustworthy and verified. This is one of the main content groups that the internal HealthCare team focuses on building and developing to share directly with readers.

Second, professional content related to diseases, symptoms, medical techniques, and treatment methods. This content is directly created, developed, and reviewed by doctors and specialists in the healthcare field. On HealthCare, we connect a network of hundreds of doctors and healthcare experts from various specialties who have worked or are currently working at major hospitals in Hanoi, Ho Chi Minh City, and across the country. They participate directly in writing, reviewing, and verifying professional content to ensure accurate, reliable, accessible, and verified medical information for readers.

How is the Handbook's Content Created?

HealthCare's Handbook focuses on sharing content and articles about Doctors, trusted medical facilities, and effective healthcare experiences for readers to reference. Shared content ensures 3 core factors: practicality, reliability, and verification. Achieving this requires a collective effort and a dedicated process.

Field Research for Experience and Data Collection

The Handbook's content creation team visits hospitals and clinics to study processes, gain real-life experiences, and gather information to share effective guidance for patients.

Consultation with Doctors, Hospitals, and Clinics

To ensure updated, accurate, and reliable professional information about doctors, hospitals, and clinics, HealthCare conducts direct consultations with medical professionals, hospitals, clinics, and healthcare facilities to verify the information.

Medical Consultation with a Professional Advisory Board

HealthCare connects with hundreds of hospitals and medical facilities with teams of doctors and specialists across various medical fields. In addition to professional discussions with medical institutions, our advisory team consists of experienced doctors and specialists in fields such as Internal Medicine, Surgery, Dermatology, Dentistry, and Musculoskeletal Disorders.

Referencing Reliable Sources

Articles reference content from reliable sources such as medical textbooks, opinions from specialist doctors, official hospital information channels, health-focused television programs, and specialized news websites.

Each Article is a Team Effort

Each piece of content and article is individually managed but represents the collective work of the entire team through a process of feedback, peer review, and thorough updates.

Professional Content on the "Healthy Living" Platform

Professional medical content on HealthCare's "Healthy Living" platform is directly written, reviewed, and verified by specialist doctors to ensure accurate, reliable, accessible, and verified medical information.
