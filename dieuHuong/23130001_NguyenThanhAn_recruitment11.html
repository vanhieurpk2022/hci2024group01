1. The 7-Step Detailed and Flexible Structure of BookingCare

The first standout feature of BookingCare's process is its clearly defined 7-step structure. From analyzing basic requirements to post-launch monitoring, each step is designed to ensure that no important aspect of the development process is overlooked. This level of detail allows the team to handle tasks systematically while maintaining the flexibility needed to respond quickly to market changes.

2. Close Coordination Between Departments

The close coordination between departments, particularly between the User Research (BA), Development (Dev), and Testing (Tester) teams, is a key factor in the product development process. Collaborating from the early stages ensures that technical requirements and testing plans are developed concurrently, providing a solid foundation for later development stages.

3. Two-Week Sprint Model

Implementing the two-week sprint model for each feature is a highlight in BookingCare's process. This not only helps maintain rapid development speed but also creates a stable work rhythm, allowing the team to focus and achieve higher efficiency in each short phase.

4. Prioritizing Features Based on Impact

BookingCare places particular emphasis on prioritizing features based on their impact. This approach ensures that the most important features, those with the greatest effect on users and business goals, are developed first. It optimizes resources and delivers quick value to the product.

5. Post-Launch Monitoring Process

We don’t end the process at the product launch stage. Post-launch monitoring demonstrates the company’s commitment to continuously improving the product. By closely tracking impact metrics and user feedback, we can quickly adjust and optimize the product, ensuring it always meets the real needs of the market.

6. Balancing Structure and Flexibility

The final difference in BookingCare's product development process is the balance between a clear structure and the necessary flexibility. While there are 7 specific steps, the process still allows for adjustments and customization to fit each specific project. This enables the team to work within an organized framework while remaining flexible enough to handle unforeseen challenges.

BookingCare's product development process is not only an effective project management tool but also a clear testament to our ongoing innovation and team spirit. Every sprint, every meeting, and every line of code we write reflects the unity and commitment of the entire team. Together, we overcome challenges, learn from our mistakes, and continuously improve to fulfill our mission of delivering the best products to our customers.
