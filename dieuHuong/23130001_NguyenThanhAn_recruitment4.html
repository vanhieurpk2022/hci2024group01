Position: Talent Acquisition Specialist

1. Job Description

  • Develop recruitment plans according to team development requirements.
  • Implement continuous recruitment programs, create candidate sources and referral channels.
  • Exploit, search, and create potential candidate databases.
  • Build networks, create relationships, work with universities to prepare human resources for the future.
  • Implement recruitment process: phone screening, information verification, interview coordination, onboarding...
  • Prepare reports, analyze, evaluate needs, and report recruitment status as required.

2. Candidate Requirements

  • 1-3 years of experience in a similar position.
  • Enthusiastic, passionate, persistent.
  • Learning spirit, effort to learn and develop oneself.
  • Proactive at work, always seeking solutions for work.
  • Good communication and relationship building skills.

Advantageous factors if available:

  • Knowledge of data analysis tools like Excel, Power BI is an advantage (training will be provided if needed).
  • Good English proficiency (reading, comprehension).

3. Working Hours

  • Monday - Friday (offline).
  • Saturday morning (online).

4. Benefits

  • Salary & Bonuses:
  • Salary range from 10 - 15 million VND/month.
  • Monthly team performance bonuses.
  • Salary review every 6 months.
