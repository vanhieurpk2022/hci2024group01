1. CFRs - Work Reporting at BookingCare

At BookingCare, we have weekly work reporting sessions, which we call CFRs. We operate based on OKRs (Objectives and Key Results). To ensure the effectiveness of OKRs, CFRs act as the fuel that drives OKRs into their trajectory.

CFRs are work reporting sessions where team members have a two-way conversation with the team lead to evaluate work progress, record results, and provide support when team members face challenges. Over 5 years of implementing OKRs, it has become clear that the lack of CFRs is a major reason for OKRs failure.

3Ps Principle in CFRs

A principle commonly applied in each CFR session is the 3Ps: Progress, Problem, and Plan/Solution. Applying these 3Ps makes the CFR session more effective, while reinforcing the commitment and accountability of each member in executing OKRs.

What is the 3Ps Method?

Progress - Work Progress/OKRs in Progress

Start the reporting session by clearly presenting the progress of your work:

  • Describe the tasks completed.
  • Summarize the goals achieved and results gained with specific data.
  • Give an overall evaluation of where the work stands compared to the original objectives.

Example: Last week, I searched for and connected with candidates for the Customer Experience (CX) position on Facebook, gathering 20 CVs. Of those, 10 candidates were interviewed in the first round, and 3 were invited to the second round.

Problem - What Difficulties Are You Facing?

After presenting the progress, point out the problems or difficulties you are facing during the work process:

  • Describe the challenges or obstacles you're encountering.
  • How are these issues affecting your work progress?
  • What have you done to solve these problems?

Example: I'm having difficulty acquiring more CVs for the CX position due to limited access to recruitment channels. To address this, I've expanded my search beyond Facebook and utilized free job sites to find more candidates.

Plan/Solution: What's Your Next Plan?

Finally, after presenting progress and problems, propose a plan to resolve the issues and continue working toward the objectives:

  • Propose solutions to resolve the issues mentioned above.
  • Describe the steps to implement the solution clearly.
  • What kind of support or guidance do you need from your superiors?

Example: To get more CVs for the position, I propose the following plan:

  • Use paid job sites like TopCV and Vieclam24h to post the job opening.
  • Introduce an internal referral policy for all company members to provide CVs.
  • Continue using the free channels to recruit candidates.
  • I will closely monitor and measure the effectiveness of paid recruitment channels and optimize them weekly.

By applying the 3Ps principle, you not only summarize your work and communicate information clearly and thoroughly, but you also make it easier to receive support and guidance from your superiors and team members when facing difficulties.

Importantly, the 3Ps approach is not only effective for implementing OKRs but can also be applied as a work habit, whether you're working with OKRs or KPIs. However, to make 3Ps truly effective, you need to prepare carefully before participating in the meetings.

Don't go into meetings empty-handed. Bring your progress, issues, and proposed solutions!
