Guidelines for Writing SEO-Friendly Content

You’re probably aware that Google regularly updates its algorithms, with each update affecting websites in various ways. In reality, Google’s goal is to provide users with the best content for each searched keyword.

At BookingCare, we don’t focus too heavily on SEO techniques. After interviewing hundreds of candidates, many are surprised to learn that we don’t have any technical SEO team. Instead, we focus on our core product—creating thousands of high-quality content pieces that offer information beyond users’ expectations. As a result, over the past 7 years, many of BookingCare's content pieces have consistently ranked at the top.

BookingCare’s SEO Content Development Process

Step 1: Choose a Topic and Keywords

Typically, we focus on long-tail keywords because they have lower competition, and the user's search intent is clearer. When you target these keywords, you’ll be able to provide very detailed content on a specific topic, aligning with the user's search goal.

Step 2: Analyze User Insights

This step is crucial as it helps you understand what users really need behind their search keywords. This insight enables you to create an article outline and detailed content that fulfills those needs.

Many people think that understanding user insights can simply be done by "putting yourself in their shoes," but that’s not enough. Insight, or deep understanding of a subject, requires more than just empathy.

At BookingCare, we use several techniques to analyze user insights:

  • Listening to and recording user feedback
  • Interviewing users
  • Experiencing the actual service usage process

Step 3: Analyze Competitors

Besides understanding your users, you also need to look at what top-ranking pages are offering—what content they provide, their article structure, and presentation. Your content needs to fulfill user needs and also outperform competitors by tenfold. This way, your article can potentially replace the current top-ranking content.

In fact, you could swap the order of steps 2 and 3. However, if you do step 3 first, you might be overly influenced by existing content.

Step 4: Create an Outline

At this stage, it becomes easier. Based on the information you've gathered, create a basic outline for your article, defining the key points you’ll elaborate on. Creating an outline ensures that you don’t miss any important ideas and helps structure your entire article effectively.

Step 5: Write the Article

As with the previous step, writing the article is straightforward. Your task is to express everything you've learned to meet user needs.

Step 6: Review and Design Visuals

Double-check several SEO factors to ensure the article meets basic SEO requirements:

  • Ensure the main keyword appears in the introduction, conclusion, and title. Don’t overuse the exact keyword—just aim to target it.
  • Ensure proper usage of H2 and H3 tags.
  • Include quotes where appropriate.
  • For images, depending on the industry, you may need design-specific images, or sometimes actual photos work better.

Step 7: Link Building

Building backlinks will help connect your article to the overall website structure. However, don’t overdo it. Choose articles that are truly related to create links that follow the user’s journey. If you include too many unrelated links, it could harm your SEO performance.

Step 8: Publish and Index with Google

After publishing the article, you need to index it with Google to ensure it appears in the search results quickly. You should understand that publishing alone doesn’t guarantee immediate visibility. Google needs to check the page, and if you notify Google of your new article, this process will be quicker.

Additionally, after publishing, sharing the article on social media platforms will help improve its ranking.

Step 9: Measure and Update

This step is often overlooked but is very important. Regularly measuring and evaluating your content helps optimize your content creation methods for your website. Over time, you’ll develop a "formula" for producing better content.

Building a 10X Content method, like the one we have now, is a long-term effort by the entire team. We acknowledge that there’s still room for improvement in BookingCare’s content, and we are continuously working to refine our methods, update our strategies, and create better content to provide users with even higher-quality information.
