About HealthCare service

This website was created with the purpose of building a synchronized system of hospitals to create favorable conditions and easily register for medical examination and treatment in a quick, convenient, and fully informative manner.

No
HealthCare is a comprehensive healthcare platform that connects users to quality, effective and reliable medical and healthcare services. HealthCare connects a network of good doctors in many different hospitals and clinics. As can be imagined, HealthCare operates according to a model similar to Uber Taxi or Grab in the field of Health - Health Care.

Collaborative partners.
BookingCare cooperates with hospitals/clinics, providing information about medical examination and treatment at the hospital/clinic to patients so that patients can easily choose the right doctor and quickly book an appointment.

BookingCare is suitable for the following patient groups:
  • The disease is not an emergency
  • Chronic diseases require examination by a specialist
  • Patients know clearly about their condition
  • Desire to proactively schedule a planned medical examination
