Company-wide Training on Essential Skills and Knowledge

We organize company-wide internal training sessions focusing on essential skills. We successfully conducted a Data Analysis Skills training session, which resulted in each team member enhancing their analytical mindset and applying data analysis in their daily tasks.

Most recently, in April, we held a Problem-Solving Skills training course—one of the top 10 essential skills for the next five years. While immediate application in work may not have been evident after the course, it was an important first step in expanding and accessing knowledge, laying the foundation for further in-depth learning and application in both work and life.

Building Small Learning Groups

In addition to company-wide training, we also establish small learning groups. These include classes on English communication, developing English reading comprehension skills, time management, emotional intelligence, and more.

The mentors of these groups are often senior colleagues with years of experience leading the classes. Each group typically lasts up to six sessions, during which members explore, discuss, and share knowledge. They then apply this knowledge in their work, with mentors providing support for any challenges encountered.

Unlike company-wide training, small learning groups encourage each member to become a guide, speaker, researcher, and sharer of knowledge within the group. Members actively participate, investing time in preparing slides and completing assignments, fostering a highly engaging and enriching environment.

Continuous Learning and Development Programs

Beyond internal training, we invite experts to teach and share specialized skills and knowledge. However, as a startup, BookingCare prioritizes developing internal training programs.

Online courses and workshops are also organized to help members access external information and stay updated on new market trends.

Everyone as a Role Model for Learning

To build a learning organization, it starts and ends with the individual’s ability to learn. We encourage everyone to be a role model for learning—continuously nurturing curiosity, enhancing skills and knowledge, and applying new learnings to their work. This spirit drives personal development and contributes to creating a dynamic workplace where learning and innovation become integral to the organization’s culture.

Regardless of the learning method, combining learning with practice is critical. We emphasize learning for practical application to achieve work effectiveness. Knowledge is only potential power; it becomes powerful when acted upon and applied. Learning, applying, refining, and relearning form a continuous, never-ending cycle. We believe that when everyone carries the flame of learning, together, we can build an organization ready to face challenges and seize opportunities in an ever-changing world.
