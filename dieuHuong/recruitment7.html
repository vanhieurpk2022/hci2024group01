AI Call Center: The Voice of Understanding from the Heart of Technology

The pioneering department in applying AI to work is the Customer Experience (CX) team, which has implemented the AI Call Center system, marking a significant step forward in optimizing customer care processes.

The team members spent nearly two months researching, selecting providers, running trials, collecting feedback, and continuously adjusting and fixing errors before officially applying the AI Call Center to the Customer Experience department at BookingCare.

Applying the AI Call Center to the appointment reminder feature not only helps improve work efficiency but also enhances customer experience, providing peace of mind for customers when booking, being reminded of appointments, and being able to actively reconnect in case of changes from medical facilities.

The initial success of the AI Call Center project, even though applied to just one feature, demonstrates the CX team’s determination to innovate and keep pace with trends. In the future, we anticipate AI will be widely applied to other aspects of the department's work, from booking appointments and answering inquiries to two-way interactions with customers. This will not only improve service quality but also affirm BookingCare's leading position in applying AI technology to provide trusted and reliable healthcare services.

AI Assistant – Your Trusted Healthcare Companion

Today, we witness a significant transformation in how people access healthcare services, from self-researching information to solutions connecting directly with specialists and healthcare services.

Understanding the growing needs of users, BookingCare introduces the AI Assistant, a pioneering product in Vietnam that supports inquiries and connects healthcare services.

The AI Assistant is integrated into both the BookingCare mobile app and website. Simply ask questions about your health, and the AI Assistant will provide answers to inquiries about symptoms, information on medical conditions, and recommend suitable healthcare services, offering users a comprehensive and personalized healthcare experience.
