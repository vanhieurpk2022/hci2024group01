About HealthCare

With passion and perseverance, our team has been and continues to develop BookingCare - Vietnam's leading comprehensive health technology platform, launched in mid-2016.

With the dream of pioneering technology to bring quality, reliable, and affordable healthcare services to the community, every family, and every individual across Vietnam.

If you are passionate and ambitious about doing meaningful things, making practical contributions to community health, for every family and every individual across Vietnam, join us.

Don’t hesitate, join the booming HealthTech field post-Covid-19 for community health. The BookingCare team looks forward to collaborating and working together with talented, experienced, and well-rounded individuals who dare to step up and make meaningful contributions.
