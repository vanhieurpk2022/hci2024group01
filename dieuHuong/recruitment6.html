About BookingCare

BookingCare is a technology startup in the Healthcare sector. To date, BookingCare has officially partnered with over 200 hospitals/clinics and supported thousands of patients in successfully booking and receiving care at specialized medical facilities in Hanoi & Ho Chi Minh City.

The BookingCare team aspires to do meaningful work, filled with enthusiasm and passion, to build a company that brings real value to customers and provides the team with continuous development opportunities, living a happy life.

We are looking for like-minded teammates in various positions to realize our vision of becoming the leading Healthcare platform in Vietnam.

Customer Experience Position

1. Job Description

  • Assist customers in booking appointments, visiting clinics, and successfully maintaining their health
  • Conduct surveys to measure and analyze customer satisfaction indices (NPS/Rule40)
  • Communicate and collaborate with partners (hospitals/clinics) to continuously enhance customer experience
  • Coordinate with departments to improve processes and methods to enhance customer experience

2. Candidate Requirements

  • Have a goal-oriented mindset for long-term development in the customer service field
  • Proactive in work, always seeking solutions
  • Team spirit, collaborating with team members and within the company
  • A learning mindset, striving for self-development

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience in customer service
  • Good English reading and comprehension skills
  • Proficiency in data analysis tools like Excel, Power BI

3. Working Hours

  • Two flexible working shifts arranged internally within the team
  • Shift 1: 7:00 AM - 4:30 PM
  • Shift 2: 8:30 AM - 6:00 PM
  • 1.5 days off per week (Saturday afternoon and Sunday)

4. Candidate Benefits

  • Salary range: 8 - 12 million VND/month
  • Monthly bonuses based on team performance
  • Periodic salary review every 6 months
