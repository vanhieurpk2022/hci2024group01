Recruitment Process for Permanent Staff

Round 1: Online Interview

After passing the CV screening stage, you will have a 30-minute conversation via Google Meet with HR. This online interview is designed for those who are interested in BookingCare but may be hesitant due to the distance, saving you time on commuting while still giving you an opportunity to learn more about BookingCare.

The goal of this round is to better understand your experience, skills, and career goals. Additionally, this is a chance for you to learn more about the position, the company (policies, scale, benefits, etc.), and help you make a more informed decision about this opportunity.

Think of this as an opportunity for a brief chat where BookingCare can learn more about you, and you can clarify any uncertainties you may have.

Round 2: In-person Interview

If in Round 1 we only met via a computer screen, in this round, you'll come directly to the company for an interview with the CEO and team leader.

The interview usually lasts between 50-60 minutes and focuses on two main areas: professional skills, work experience, and cultural fit. In addition to technical skills, we place particular importance on finding candidates who align with BookingCare's work culture.

As a startup, we are continuously building our culture and core values. Every member is seen as an essential piece in the company’s overall picture. There is no unnecessary piece, but the alignment of each piece is key to achieving a perfect whole. Just like a painting with many colors, it needs to be harmonious. We believe that success comes not just from individual abilities but also from how well each individual fits into the company culture.

This round is not only an opportunity for us to learn more about you, but also for you to better understand the job and the culture at BookingCare. From the moment you step into the company, or throughout the interview, you'll get a feel for the working environment and whether our team is a good fit for you.

We strongly encourage you to ask questions to better understand the job and development opportunities at BookingCare. Consider this an open conversation where you can not only answer questions but also ask your own, share, and be heard. So, if you have any questions after Round 1, don't hesitate to ask to get a more comprehensive view of the job and growth opportunities at BookingCare.

A cohesive, like-minded team is the core factor in building a successful company. That’s why BookingCare is always on the lookout for talented, passionate individuals who are ready to join us on our journey to provide quality healthcare services and valuable health information to the people of Vietnam. Join the BookingCare team and help create meaningful value for the community!
