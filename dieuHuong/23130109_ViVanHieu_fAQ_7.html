Customer benefits

The appointment booking system helps patients easily choose the right doctor and make an appointment quickly.
  • Network of skilled, reputable specialists with verified information
  • Doctor's appointment schedule on the system in real time of the doctor at the medical facility (hospital, clinic)
  • See the exact doctor the patient has chosen to schedule an appointment with
  • Prestigious and reliable medical facilities, reasonable, clear and transparent medical examination and treatment costs (We do not cooperate with unprestigious medical facilities).
  • Support before, during and after your visit
  • Protect patient rights when going to the doctor.
  • Free re-examination

    • After the examination, if the patient is not satisfied with the examination process, consultation and treatment plan of the doctor, the system will support the patient to meet the doctor again for a more detailed examination and consultation (if the patient requests and is appropriate). The patient is supported with a free examination with another doctor in the same specialty (if the patient's request is appropriate).
HealthCare does not directly provide medical examination and treatment services, but acts as a link between patients and doctors. In practice, it is inevitable that problems will arise. Because of our trusted partnership with doctors and medical facilities, we will support patients in resolving their concerns thoroughly. In fact, many patients' questions have been clearly answered or supported with free re-examination.

Yes, you will be examined by the doctor who has scheduled an appointment. In practice, doctors can change their appointment schedule suddenly for some reason, especially good doctors who are often very busy. However, BookingCare always closely monitors the doctor's appointment schedule to update and notify promptly as well as minimize errors.

Make an appointment in advance to be scheduled with the doctor you have chosen. If you do not make an appointment, you may not be able to see the doctor you want. Through HealthCare, you will receive:
  • Get support before, during, and after your visit
  • Know that doctor information has been clearly and transparently verified
  • See the exact doctor the patient has chosen to schedule an appointment with
  • Minimize waiting time to see a doctor
  • HealthCare protects your rights when you go to the doctor.
  • In addition, BookingCare offers a "free" medical examination booking service. You pay the medical examination and treatment costs directly at the hospital or clinic.
  • Free re-examination
After the examination, if you are not satisfied with the examination process, consultation and treatment plan of the doctor, the system will support you to meet the doctor again for a more detailed examination and consultation (if you have a request and it is appropriate). You will be supported with a free examination with another doctor in the same specialty (if your request is appropriate).
